## Making Investments

### Investment Tiers

Phonix offers three investment tiers with different monthly returns:

| Tier | Investment Range | Monthly Return | Daily Income |
|------|------------------|----------------|--------------|
| **Tier 1** | $50 - $3,000 | 6% | ~0.2% |
| **Tier 2** | $3,001 - $5,000 | 8% | ~0.27% |
| **Tier 3** | $5,001+ | 10% | ~0.33% |

### How to Purchase a Subscription

1. **Navigate to Investment**
   - Click "Investment" in the menu
   - Click "Purchase Subscription"

2. **Choose Your Amount**
   - Minimum: $50
   - Maximum: Your available balance
   - The tier is automatically calculated

3. **Use Registration Bonus (Optional)**
   - Check "Use Registration Bonus"
   - Up to 10% of investment (max $1)
   - Example: $100 investment = $1 bonus usable

4. **Confirm Purchase**
   - Review details
   - Click "Purchase"
   - Your subscription activates immediately!

### Understanding Your Subscription

Each subscription has:

- **Investment Amount**: What you paid
- **Tier**: Auto-calculated (1, 2, or 3)
- **Monthly Rate**: Return percentage
- **Daily Income**: Paid Monday-Friday
- **Earnings Cap**: 3x your investment
- **Days Active**: How long it's been earning
- **Total Earned**: Current earnings
- **Remaining Capacity**: How much more it can earn

### Example Investment

**Scenario**: You invest $1,000

- **Tier**: Tier 1 (6% monthly)

**Monthly Breakdown**:
- Daily earnings: ~$2.00 (Mon-Fri only)

- Plus referral commissions if you build a team!

## Wallet Management

### Your Wallet

You have two balance types:

1. **Main Wallet**: USDT balance for investments/withdrawals

### Making a Deposit

**Step 1: Get Your Deposit Address**
1. Navigate to Wallet ‚Üí Deposit
2. Copy your unique TRC20 address
3. Or scan the QR code

**Step 2: Send USDT (TRC20)**
1. Open your external wallet (e.g., Trust Wallet, Binance)
2. Send USDT to your deposit address
3. **Important**: Only send TRC20 USDT (TRON network)
4. Wait for blockchain confirmation (1-3 minutes)

**Step 3: Automatic Credit**
- Deposits are monitored every minute
- Balance updates automatically after confirmation
- You'll see a transaction in your history

> ‚ö†Ô∏è **Warning**: Only send TRC20 USDT! Other tokens or networks will be lost.

**Deposit Tips**:
- Minimum deposit: $10 recommended
- Always double-check the address
- Save a screenshot of transaction hash
- Deposits usually take 1-5 minutes

### Making a Withdrawal

**Requirements**:
- Minimum withdrawal: $10
- Must set withdrawal address in profile first
- 5% withdrawal fee applies

**Step 1: Set Withdrawal Address**
1. Go to Profile
2. Enter your TRC20 wallet address
3. Save

**Step 2: Request Withdrawal**
1. Navigate to Wallet ‚Üí Withdraw
2. Enter amount
3. Review 5% fee calculation
4. Confirm withdrawal

**Step 3: Admin Approval**
- All withdrawals require admin approval
- Usually processed within 24 hours
- Track status in transaction history

**Step 4: Receive Funds**
- Once approved, funds sent to blockchain
- Check TronScan with transaction hash
- Usually arrives in 1-3 minutes

**Withdrawal Example**:
- Withdraw: $100
- Fee (5%): $5
- You receive: $95
- Transaction recorded in history

## Rank System

### Rank Overview

Ranks are based on **leg volumes** - total investments in your referral legs.

### Rank Tiers

| Rank | Requirements | Weekly Bonus | Duration |
|------|--------------|--------------|----------|
| **Connector** | Main: $5K, Other: $5K | $50/week | 52 weeks |
| **Builder** | Main: $10K, Other: $10K | $200/week | 52 weeks |
| **Professional** | Main: $20K, Other: $20K | $500/week | 52 weeks |
| **Executive** | Main: $50K, Other: $50K | $1,000/week | 52 weeks |
| **Director** | Main: $100K, Other: $100K | $2,000/week | 52 weeks |
| **Crown** | Main: $200K, Other: $200K | $5,000/week | 52 weeks |

### Understanding Leg Volumes

**What are Legs?**
- Each direct referral creates a "leg"
- A leg includes all investments down that line

**Example**:

You

‚îú‚îÄ‚îÄ Leg 2 (Bob's team): $15,000 total volume ‚Üê Main Leg
‚îî‚îÄ‚îÄ Leg 3 (Charlie's team): $5,000 total volume

- **Main Leg**: $15,000 (largest)
- **Other Legs**: $8,000 + $5,000 = $13,000

**Rank Check**: 
- Main: $15K, Other: $13K
- ‚úÖ Qualifies for **Builder** ($10K/$10K)
- ‚ùå Not yet **Professional** (needs $20K/$20K)

### Achieving Ranks

**How Ranks Are Awarded**:
1. Leg volumes calculated weekly (Sunday night)
2. System checks all users for qualification
3. If you qualify, rank is awarded Monday morning
4. Weekly bonus starts immediately
5. Continues for 52 weeks

**When Bonuses Stop**:
- After 52 weeks of payments
- If you reach your 3x user cap
- Rank must be re-achieved to restart bonus

### Rank Strategies

**To Achieve Higher Ranks**:

1. **Build Multiple Legs**
   - Don't focus on just one leg
   - Spread referrals across 2-3 legs
   - Helps meet "other legs" requirement

2. **Support Your Team**
   - Help directs recruit their own teams
   - Teach them the system
   - Active teams = bigger volumes

3. **Lead by Example**
   - Stay actively invested
   - Show results
   - Be available for questions

4. **Track Progress**
   - Check leg volumes in dashboard
   - See how close you are to next rank
   - Identify which legs need growth

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Is Phonix a pyramid scheme?**  
A: No. Phonix is a legitimate MLM investment platform with real products (investment packages) and 20 levels of commissions based on actual team performance.

**Q: How do I make money?**  
A: Three ways:
1. Daily bond income from your subscriptions (passive)
2. Commissions from team investments (active)
3. Weekly rank bonuses (achievement-based)

**Q: What is the minimum investment?**  
A: $50 minimum per subscription.

**Q: Can I have multiple subscriptions?**  
A: Yes! You can purchase as many subscriptions as your balance allows.

### Investment Questions

**Q: What happens when my subscription reaches 3x?**  
A: It stops earning daily income. You keep all earnings, but that specific subscription becomes inactive.

**Q: Can I withdraw my principal investment?**  
A: No, investments are locked in subscriptions. You withdraw your earnings (commissions, daily income, bonuses).

**Q: Which tier should I choose?**  
A:
- Start small ($50-$100) to learn the system
- Higher tiers = better rates but more risk
- Tier 3 (10%) is best ROI but requires $5,001+

**Q: When do I receive daily income?**  
A: Every day Monday-Friday at 12:00 AM UTC. Weekends do not count.

### Referral Questions

**Q: Do I need a big team to make money?**  
A: No! Even small teams generate commissions. Focus on quality over quantity.

**Q: Why aren't I earning from Level 5?**  
A: Check your direct referral count. Level 5+ requires at least 3 direct referrals.

**Q: Can I change my upline/referrer?**  
A: No, the referral relationship is permanent once set.

**Q: What if I registered without a referral code?**  
A: You'll still earn from your own downline, but you won't have an upline earning from you.

### Wallet Questions

**Q: How long do deposits take?**  
A: Usually 1-5 minutes after blockchain confirmation. Maximum 10 minutes.

**Q: Why is there a 5% withdrawal fee?**  
A: Covers blockchain gas fees and platform operations.

**Q: Can I cancel a withdrawal?**  
A: Only if it's still "Pending" and not yet approved by admin.

**Q: What if I sent wrong token type?**  
A: Only TRC20 USDT is supported. Other tokens cannot be recovered. Always double-check!

### Rank Questions

**Q: How often are ranks checked?**  
A: Weekly, every Sunday night at 11:00 PM UTC.

**Q: Can I lose my rank?**  
A: No, once achieved it stays for 52 weeks. After that, you must re-qualify.

**Q: What if I hit my 3x cap during rank period?**  
A: Weekly bonuses stop even if you have weeks remaining. The rank status stays.

**Q: Do rank bonuses count toward 3x cap?**  
A: Yes, all earnings (daily income, commissions, bonuses) count toward your cap.

### 3x Cap Questions

**Q: What counts toward my 3x cap?**  
A: All earnings:
- Daily bond income
- Referral commissions
- Weekly rank bonuses
- Registration bonus usage

**Q: What happens when I reach 3x cap?**  
A: All earnings stop. To earn again, you must invest more (which raises your cap).

**Q: How do I increase my cap?**  
A: Make new investments. Each $1 invested increases your cap by $3.

**Q: Is the cap per subscription or per user?**  
A: Both!
- Each subscription has its own 3x cap (for daily income)
- You as a user have a global 3x cap (for all earnings)

### Technical Questions

**Q: What blockchain do you use?**  
A: TRON blockchain with TRC20 USDT tokens.

**Q: Is my private key secure?**  
A: Yes, all deposit address private keys are encrypted. The platform never asks for your withdrawal wallet's private key.

**Q: Can I use the platform on mobile?**  
A: Yes, the website is mobile-responsive. A dedicated mobile app may be released in the future.

**Q: How do I contact support?**  
A: Email admin@phonix.com or use the contact form in your dashboard.
